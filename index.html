Interests & Hobbies

Music

I enjoy a variety of music genres, but rap and hip-hop have a special place for me. Some of my favorite artists include Kendrick Lamar, J. Cole, and Travis Scott. Their lyricism, storytelling, and production are top-notch, and they all bring a different vibe to their music. Kendrick's deep, socially-conscious bars, J. Cole's introspective flow, and Travis Scott's futuristic soundscapes make for a powerful blend.

Sports

I’ve always been passionate about soccer, both playing and following the sport closely. I play regularly as a midfielder, and I enjoy the balance between offense and defense in that position. Being able to control the tempo of the game and link up play between the back line and the forwards is something I love. As for teams, I’m a huge fan of FC Barcelona. Their style of play, the famous tiki-taka approach, and their history of developing world-class talent through La Masia make them a fascinating team to follow.

Travel

Traveling has always been something I’m passionate about because it’s an incredible way to experience new cultures, people, and landscapes. One of the most memorable trips I’ve taken was to Spain. I visited cities like Barcelona, Madrid, and Seville. In Barcelona, walking down La Rambla and seeing Gaudí’s masterpiece, La Sagrada Família, was absolutely breathtaking. The mix of modernist architecture with rich historical sites like Park Güell made it a perfect blend of culture and beauty. And of course, catching a game at Camp Nou was a dream come true for a Barcelona fan like me.
