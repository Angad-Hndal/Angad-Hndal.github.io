About Me

Hello! I’m Angad Hundal, a Computer Science student at Simon Fraser University with a keen interest in numerical analysis and applied mathematics. Outside the classroom, I stay active in my community through volunteer work with the Surrey Crime Prevention Society and the Adopt a Street Program— experiences that have strengthened my passion for service and teamwork.

When I’m not studying or volunteering, you’ll find me playing soccer, discovering new music, or planning my next travel adventure. I’m driven by a desire to use technology and math-driven solutions to make a positive impact on the world around me.

Sports

Travel

Traveling has always been something I’m passionate about because it’s an incredible way to experience new cultures, people, and landscapes. One of the most memorable trips I’ve taken was to Spain. I visited cities like Barcelona, Madrid, and Seville. In Barcelona, walking down La Rambla and seeing Gaudí’s masterpiece, La Sagrada Família, was absolutely breathtaking. The mix of modernist architecture with rich historical sites like Park Güell made it a perfect blend of culture and beauty. And of course, catching a game at Camp Nou was a dream come true for a Barcelona fan like me.
